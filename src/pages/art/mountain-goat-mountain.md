# Mountain Goat Mountain

Mountain Goat Mountain was a Q\*Bert meets Crossy Road arcade game for iOS, Apple TV and Occulus published by Zynga.
I art directed the game and contributed concept art, 3D modeling, UI, and VFX.

## Concepts

The game included unlockable goats which would also change the environment you played in.
For many of these new areas, I concepted the rough color-composition and feel for the area before diving into modeling

## Look Development

We explored many styles before landing on what became Mountain Goat Mountain.
One iteration was a more faceted clay-rendered look which was appealing, but didn't have the fun factor we were looking for
